COLOR BUILDER

Welcome to Color Builder!

Follow these simple steps to create your custom color scheme:

  1. Select a Color: Use the color picker to choose your initial color. This will be the base for your first swatch.
  2. Create a Swatch: Click or tap on the swatch display area below the color picker to create a new swatch with your selected color.
  3. Manipulate Swatches: Drag and drop swatches within the display area to organize your palette. Swatches that touch will automatically generate a gradient in the gradient display area.
  4. Pick From Gradient: Click on the gradient display to select a color from the generated gradient. Your chosen color will be shown in the selected color display box.
  5. Adjust Swatch Colors: Right-click on a swatch to open the context menu. Choose 'Change Color' to adjust the swatch's color or 'Delete Swatch' to remove it.
  6. Experiment and Explore: Feel free to experiment with different colors and swatch arrangements. The Color Builder tool is designed for playful exploration and discovery of perfect color schemes.
